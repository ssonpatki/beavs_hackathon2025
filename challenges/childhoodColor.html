The Color of Childhood

Close your eyes and drift back to the earliest hues of memory — the golden glow of summer afternoons, the soft blue hush of bedtime stories, the wild splashes of laughter and scraped knees. In this challenge, we invite you to paint not just with pigment, but with feeling. What color held your hand as you grew? What shade cradled your dreams? Through brushstroke or digital bloom, capture the essence of your beginnings. Let your childhood speak… in color.
